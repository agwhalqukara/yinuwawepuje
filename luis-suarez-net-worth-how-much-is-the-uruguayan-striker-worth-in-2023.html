    Who is Luis Suarez?

    Luis Suarez was born on January 24, 1987, in Salto, Uruguay.

    He grew up in a poor family with seven siblings.

    His father left the family when he was nine years old, and his mother struggled to make ends meet.

    Suarez started playing football on the streets and joined a local club called Urreta.

    He was discovered by a scout from Nacional, one of the biggest clubs in Uruguay when he was 12 years old.

    Luis made his debut for Nacional in 2005, at the age of 18, and scored 10 goals in 27 appearances.

    Suarez joined Barcelona in 2014, won four La Liga titles, and scored 195 goals in 283 games.

    In 2020, he helped Atletico Madrid win their first La Liga title since 2014.

    Suarez is Uruguay’s all-time top scorer with 68 goals in 137 games, won the Copa America title in 2011, and represented his country at four FIFA World Cup tournaments and four Copa America tournaments.

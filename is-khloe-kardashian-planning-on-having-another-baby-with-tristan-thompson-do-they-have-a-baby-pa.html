    Khloe Kardashian and Tristan Thompson share their sweet, one-year-old daughter True Thompson together. Now, new rumors are suggesting that Khloe wants another baby so badly, she’s willing to make a “baby pact” with Tristan Thompson. The agreement would mean that True has another sibling who shares the same parents. It’s a common rumor that plagues the Kardashian/Jenner family. It was once rumored that Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick were going to have another baby and the rumor is already circulating around Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott.

    So far, Khloe Kardashian hasn’t said anything publicly that would indicate a baby pact exists, and instead, she has made multiple public comments to the contrary.

    Khloe has made it clear that she’s focusing on True Thompson right now and isn’t even looking for a relationship. She hasn’t given any indications she is ready to go through pregnancy and childbirth a second time.

    Some people don’t believe that Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick had an on-again-off-again relationship but think that some of her children were actually born as an agreement. This rumor has been online for years, and there are even rumors that suggest she will have a fourth child with Scott in the same manner.

    For all of these rumors, there has been little to no evidence.

    Kim and Kourtney are the only siblings who have more than one child, and each child and his or her siblings share the same parents.

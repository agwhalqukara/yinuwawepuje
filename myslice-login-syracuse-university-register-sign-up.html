    MySlice Login Syracuse University

    MySlice is the official student portal of Syracuse University. MySlice is used to access student information, grades, and other resources.

    MySlice is also used to register for classes, view your financial aid information, track your academic progress and more.

    MySlice Register & Sign Up

    • Go to the MySlice site and click Register.
    • Enter your SUID and password, then click Log In.
    • If you have not yet provided your SSN or a tax ID number (TIN), enter it now in the appropriate field, then click Save Changes.

    Syracuse University New Student Registration

    You will be able to register for classes, add/drop a course and see your schedule once the registration process is complete. You can access the registration website by visiting the Student Center tab on mySlice or by clicking here: [mySlice] (http://www.syr.edu/online/.cblibrary/lib-web/studentcenter/?resource=resourcestitle&1). Please note that you will need to use your SUID and password to log in.

    If you have any questions regarding this registration process please contact us at [email protected]

    Syracuse University MySlice Login Guide

    To login to your account, you need to enter your username and password. If you don’t know them, follow the steps below to retrieve them:

    • Go to https://myslice.syr.edu/
    • Click on the Login button at the top of the screen
    • Enter your University ID number in the box provided and click Submit Your Information

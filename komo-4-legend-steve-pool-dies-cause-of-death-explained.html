    It is with profound sorrow that we share the passing of a Seattle icon, Steve Pool. The legendary weathercaster, who graced the screens of KOMO 4 for over four decades, has left us this week. His departure leaves a void in the hearts of many who admired his dedication and warmth.

    Steve Pool was more than just a meteorologist; he was a trusted companion to the people of Seattle. His daily weather reports were filled not only with accurate forecasts but also with an infectious enthusiasm that radiated through the screen, making even the dreariest Seattle days seem a little brighter.

    Over the years, Steve’s commitment to his craft earned him a special place in the hearts of viewers. His authenticity and sense of humor made him relatable, while his dedication to his profession made him a trustworthy source of information. It was these qualities that transformed him from a weather forecaster into a community figure.

    In 2019, Steve announced his retirement following a battle with prostate cancer. Despite the challenges he faced, he overcame above everything, but later on Alzheimer disease caught his body & became the reason of his death as said by his wife in facebook post.

    Even in retirement, Steve continued to leave an indelible mark on the community. His journey, chronicled through various platforms, served as a beacon of hope for those facing similar struggles. His story reminded us all of the power of resilience, the importance of community support, and the strength found in positivity.

    Today, we remember Steve Pool not just as a dedicated weatherman but as a friend, a mentor, and an inspiration. His legacy will live on in the hearts of the countless individuals he touched throughout his remarkable career. His contributions to the field of meteorology, and more importantly, to the community he served, will never be forgotten.

    The news of Steve’s passing is undoubtedly heartbreaking. But as we mourn his loss, let’s also celebrate his life – a life marked by service, courage, and an unwavering commitment to his community. Let’s remember him for the joy he brought into our living rooms every evening, for the reassurance he provided in times of stormy weather, and for the enduring impact he left on Seattle’s broadcasting landscape.

    Steve Pool was a true legend. His memory will continue to inspire, and his legacy will remain a testament to his extraordinary life. Our deepest condolences go out to his family, friends, and all those mourning his loss. Rest in peace, Steve Pool. You will be missed.

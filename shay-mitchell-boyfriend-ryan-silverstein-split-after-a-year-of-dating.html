    No more play with Shay! Pretty Little Liars star Shay Mitchell has split from her boyfriend Ryan Silverstein, multiple sources confirm to Us Weekly.

    The ABC Family actress, 27, and Silverstein were dating for about one year before they split. One insider told Us the two fought frequently throughout the course of their relationship.

    Two separate sources tell Us that Mitchell, who's unattached these days, has been coping with her split well and enjoying the perks of singlehood. "She's definitely having fun," one insider says of the PLL star, who plays Emily on the smash drama.

    The same source adds that Mitchell recently did a photo shoot with model Gui Fedrizzi in Malibu, and the two have been talking every since. "They flirted all day on set and left together at the end of the day," the insider says.

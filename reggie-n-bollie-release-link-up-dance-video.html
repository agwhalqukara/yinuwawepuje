    14 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Former 2015 X Factor stars Reggie ‘N’ Bollie left their record label Syco earlier this month, just months after releasing their latest single ‘Link Up‘.

    The track that features a sample of Peter Andre’s mega hit ‘Mysterious Girl‘ is a huge fan favourite and the Ghanaian stars have finally released a music video for the hit song.

    The official clip is a dance video featuring a group of female dancers dancing to the upbeat track. Reggie ‘N’ Bollie do not appear in the clip.

    The fun music video is Reggie ‘N’ Bollie’s first release through their new independent label F.R.O.D Music which has been set up by the pair since parting ways with Simon Cowell’s record label Syco.

    This month will also see RnB as they are known to fans around the world, releasing a brand new single. The as-yet untitled track is set to feature fellow ex X Factor stars Rough Copy.

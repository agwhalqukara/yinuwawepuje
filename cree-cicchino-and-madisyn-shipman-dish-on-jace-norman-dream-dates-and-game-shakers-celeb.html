    One of our favorite Nickelodeon shows — Game Shakers — is back with a brand new episode tonight and our gals Cree Cicchino and Madisyn Shipman are telling us ALL about it!

    For Nickelodeon’s Crush Night, the episode will follow Babe (Cicchino) and how she tries to plan the perfect date when she finds out that Henry Hart (Jace Norman) is coming to New York to visit her.

    In the Q&A below, the girls talk about having Jace on the Game Shakers set, their dream dates with their celebrity crushes, and what we can expect from upcoming episodes (HINT — a LOT of romance!).

    Make sure to catch “Babe Loves Danger” tomorrow, February 10th @ 8:30PM ET/PT on Nickelodeon, as well as new Game Shakers episodes every Sunday at 7PM.

    Celeb Secrets: Game Shakers returns this Saturday with new episodes! What’s to come from Babe, Kenzie, and the rest of the GS this season?

    Cree Cicchino: “Some dancing, rapping, a little romance, a bunch of sillies, all the fun stuff you’d expect from the Game Shakers.”

    Madisyn Shipman: “There is a lot of boy drama this season! Our character have changed a lot over the years and we are definitely growing up which is awesome to see throughout the episodes!”

    CS: On Saturday, Jace Norman guest stars as his character Henry. I know you’ve worked with Jace before for your crossover, but what was it like having him back and guest starring on your show?

    CC: “It was great! Jace is super talented and we had a lot of fun. It’s always nice to work with a good friend.”

    MS: “It’s great working with Jace. He is a super nice guy and always has great advice when you need it! We do film in the same building as the HD kids but we don’t really get to see them often due to our different schedules.”

    CS: In the episode, Babe tries to plan the perfect date with Henry. What’s your ideal date with your crush?

    CC: “I don’t know probably just sitting at home drinking tea and being anti-social together. Knitting may or may not be involved.”

    MS: “My ideal date is going to a theme park, then to the beach to watch the sunset. I feel the theme park brings fun and character into the mist then going to the beach and watching the sunset is romantic yet beautiful.”

    CS: We know that the girls are creating a dating app this season. Does this mean we will we see romance for Babe and Kenzie in these upcoming episodes?

    CC: “Yeah that’ll definitely happen. There’s actually a whole episode about some dating app mishaps.”

    MS: “I can’t reveal too much but like I said the girls are definitely growing up and boys are being introduced.”

    CS: It seems like the GS are going to get into a lot of crazy situations these upcoming episodes — which one is your favorite (that you could tease) and why?

    CC: “I can’t really spoil anything but in one upcoming episode, Babe has a fear of hers revealed that happens to be one of my real life fears as well. It was super fun to play around with that.”

    MS: “Ok this is easy! Romeo Beckham! Romeo has been my celebrity crush for about 4 years now and when he followed me on Instagram I freaked out! I will gladly be his Juliet.”

    CS: What’s one thing you want your characters to do (or achieve) in upcoming episodes?

    CC: “It’s not something I necessarily want to achieve, but I think it would be really fun to meet Babe’s mom.”

    MS: “We have a lot of episodes including a very special guest coming up! I can’t reveal too much but he’s connected to GG in some way. You’ll have to watch the upcoming episodes to find out who!!”

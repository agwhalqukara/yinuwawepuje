    19 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Khloé Kardashian looked edgy and chic late on Friday when she was spotted arriving at the club The Nice Guy in West Hollywood.

    The 37-year-old Keeping Up With The Kardashians star was attired in sleek all-black leather items as she made a grand entrance to the club, where Justin Bieber was throwing an afterparty following a nearby performance.

    The club was thrown into chaos in the early morning hours of Saturday when gunfire broke out while rappers Kodak Black, Gunna and Lil Baby were standing outside, which left three men with gunshot wounds, the Los Angeles Police Department confirmed to DailyMail.com.

    Shocking turn of event: Khloé Kardashian, 37, stunned in an all-black leather ensemble as she entered Justin Bieber’s afterparty at West Hollywood The Nice Guy on Friday night. Later, gunshots erupted outside near rappers Kodak Black, Gunna and Lil Baby

    Khloé looked stunning in a short black leather dress that hugged her curves and highlighted her toned tummy.

    She paired her dress with floppy thigh-high black leather boots and she covered up with a sleek black duster that reached down to her ankles. 

    The reality star accessorized with a small black alligator-print handbag, and she wore her voluminous blond locks in a high ponytail that was fanned out down her shoulders.

    She practiced safety and wore a black mask as she entered, in accordance with California’s indoor mask mandate.

    Back in black: Khloé stunned in a black leather dress with thigh-high boots and a long duster, as well as a slim black handbag

    Khloe was on the guest list for the star-studded bash, which Justin hosted following a pre-Super Bowl performance with Drake at the Pacific Design Center.

    Rappers Kodak Black, Lil Baby and Gunna were leaving the party around 2:45 a.m. when a fight broke out with the rappers’ friends who were in a red Ferrari Sports Car, TMZ reported.

    Three men were wounded and all are said to be in stable condition, with the gunman still at large.

    N’Golo Kante is a French professional footballer who currently plays for the English club Chelsea and the France national team as a defensive midfielder. He began his junior career with JS Suresnes in 1999 and made his senior debut with Boulogne in 2012. From 2013 to 2015, he was a member of Caen, and from 2015 to 2016, he was a member of Leicester City. Kante was a key midfielder in Leicester’s first-ever Premier League title-winning squad in 2015-16. With Chelsea, he won the Premier League in 2016-17 and the FA Cup in 2017-18. He also became the first outfield player since Eric Cantona in 1992 and 1993 to win back-to-back English Premier League titles with different clubs.

    In 2016, Kante made his senior international debut for France. He was a key member of France’s national team, which finished second in the 2016 Euro Cup and won the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia.
